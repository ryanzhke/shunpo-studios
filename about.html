I'm a freelance multidisciplinary designer and self-taught photographer based in Raleigh, North Carolina.

I started drawing graphical paintings and applications in 2014 and since then have created many works of art. My highly adaptive skill set spans painting, illustration, story writing, character and concept art, graphic and web design, and 2D animation.

During my eight years of experience, I had the chance to work on a variety of topics; luxury, lifestyle, food, travel, health. As a digital freelance designer, I collaborate with inspiring brands, agencies, and startups to shape their projects and create intuitive experiences.

This website showcases my recent projects with a focus on areas spanning from simplistic aesthetics to contemporary abstractions.

Shunpo Studios was founded to encompass the same high-quality production while providing a platform to collaborate with other like-minded creatives.
